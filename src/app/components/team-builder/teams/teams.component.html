Team Builder

The Team Builder is used to create not only the teams you currently have, but also the teams you wish you had.

Add champions to a new team to easily see what they bring to the table at a glance. This will allow for being able to theorycraft a bit easier and in a more visual way.

1. To get started, just enter a team name right about the link you clicked to read this, then press enter, or click the + icon.

2. After your new team is added, you'll see it appear on the screen with an Add and Edit button.

3. Clicking the Add button will bring you to the Champions page. Simply search for and click the champion to add.

4. On the Champion Details page, if you are on a desktop computer, you will see a + button in the top left inside the navigation bar. Click this to add this champion to your team. On mobile, this button will be at the bottom of your screen.

5. Profit.
